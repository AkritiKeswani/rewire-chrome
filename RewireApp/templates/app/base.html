Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? You might find an answer here! Can't find all your answers? Feel free to contact us.

  • Do I need to make an account to start?

    In order to reap the benefits of using Rewire, it is definitely a must! This is simply because you can log in and track progress of your focus session, and see where it ends or breaks in the middle. For monetary incentives, in order to gain money from another user that you nominated, in the event of them stopping their focus session, this is necessary!

  • Where can I find more information about the research associated with Rewire?

    There are some studies posted on PubMed/NCBI as well as universitiy webpages. Some of these include Multicosts of Multitasking (PubMed) and Why Are We Distracted by Social Media? Distraction Situations and Strategies, Reasons for Distraction, and Individual Differences (Frontiers in Psychology).

  • How can Rewire help me stay focused on my work without switching between tasks on my laptop?

    Rewire tracks your sessions on the web whenever you enable the extension, so your progress in terms of time in a focus session is tracked, and when you switch to another tab, you are navigated to another page that motivates you to go back and complete your session, minimizing distractions such as social media sites!

  • What makes this different than other focus-enhancing applications out there?

    The mission of Rewire is not to sell some sophisticated pitch for a focus-enhancing application that is super complex to use! The motive here mainly is to emphasize how distractions minimize progress in any task we aim to complete, and sites such as social media platforms could not only affect work progress while in the middle of a task, but can also impact mental health detrimentally by making a person conscious about too many things at once. The goal here is to raise awareness and help you achieve your productvity goals through a seamlessly integrated web extension that is low-maintenance!

  • Ok, I am sold on this! What do I do next?

    By clicking on the "Get Started" button, you can navigate to the download page where you can install the extension and register to begin using the platform!
